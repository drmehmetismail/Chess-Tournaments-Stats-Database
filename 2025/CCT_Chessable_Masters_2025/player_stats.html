What is game intelligence (GI)? The GI score combines human performance with engine analysis, measuring the players' ability to take strategic risks. The GI score increases with winning more points and scoring against stronger opposition, but it decreases with mistakes. The average chess player's GI score is 100. About 68% of the players have a GI score between 85 and 115. Super tournament winners typically achieve a GI score of 160 or above.
